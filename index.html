Currently I am a third year Ph.D. student of Department of Computer Science and Technology at Nanjing University and a member of LAMDA Group, led by professor Zhi-Hua Zhou. I received the M.S. degree from Nanjing University in Jun. 2019 and the B.E. degree from Sun Yat-sen University in Jun. 2016.

Supervisor

Professor Lijun Zhang.

Research Interest

My research interests include machine learning and optimization. My main focus is on the development of efficient algorithms with novel and provable performance guarantees for online learning.
